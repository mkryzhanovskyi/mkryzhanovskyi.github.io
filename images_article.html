Logo Image Restrictions in Agiloft

In order to use the image as a logo it needs to meet the following criteria:

Max width of uploaded image: 500px

Max height of uploaded image: 300px

Max size of uploaded image: 200,000 bytes (200KB or 0.2MB)

Workaround: It is possible to override the settings above. Under “Body” tab, in the Look&Feel settings, for Background settings choose: “Image: Picture from file” and upload any image. This will also allow to choose this image as a logo under “General” tab.

PLEASE NOTE: the workaround does not work in the newest Agiloft releases and all logo images must comply with the restrictions above.
