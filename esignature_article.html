DocuSign and Adobe Sign: Message to Signer

In order to add a unique message with a DocuSign or Adobe Sign envelope proceed to Setup -> Integrations -> Adobe Sign/ DocuSign Extension.

Under “Action” choose Contract.Create Adobe Sign Envelope or Contract.Create DocuSign Envelope and click “Edit Action”.
